ABOUT US

Our story started in January of 2019. As news spread about the forest fires in the Amazon rainforest, our team decided to take action. We had hundreds of volunteers flown down to help the displaced familes, the injured animals, and to bring supplies. But forest fires are not the only enemy. The forest is being destroyed to make space for agriculture, animal grazing, manufacturing and construction.

Our mission is to save the forests by buying the land it resides on. This is made possible soley through your donations(because Save the Forest is a non-profit organization). With your help, we are able to purchase these acres and register them as privately owned making them unaccessable to anyone with ill intentions.

Our work is not focused on only one area. We have acquired land all over the globe, including forests and jungles in Belize,Costa Rica, Hawaii, Africa, Asia, and parts of Central America. If you would like to visit any of these areas, please visit the locations section to learn more.
